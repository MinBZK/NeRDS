# About the Dutch Guideline for Digital Systems

The Dutch Guideline for Digital Systems (NeRDS, pronounced as _de /nɛrts/_) is a set of standards, guidelines and
practical tools for
responsibly developing, procuring and using digital systems within the Dutch government.

In the Dutch Guideline for Digital Systems you will find not only information about designing, building and
implementing technology, but also concrete tools and utilities that you can directly apply in your daily
work.

The information is intended for employees, civil servants, of the national government, provinces, municipalities and water boards.

## Government information

The Dutch Guideline for Digital Systems is a website of the Ministry of the Interior and
Kingdom Relations. The Bureau Architectuur Nederlandse Overheid (BANO) compiles the information.

## Core guidelines

The Dutch Guideline for Digital Systems helps government organizations make better choices when
designing, building and implementing technology.
By applying this guideline and using our practical tools and utilities, you ensure that your
digital services:

1. Are **responsibly** designed
2. **Meet legal requirements** including the BIO
3. Are **inclusive and accessible** for all citizens
4. Are **transparent and explainable** in their operation
5. Are **secure and reliable** throughout their entire lifecycle

## Who is this guideline for?

This guideline has been developed for everyone involved in designing, developing, procuring, implementing or
managing digital systems within the Dutch government:

- **Policy makers** who formulate digital strategies
- **Project leaders** who manage digital projects
- **Developers** and **Architects** who build digital systems
- **Purchasers** who select digital solutions
- **Lawyers** who monitor legal compliance

## Use this guideline

Use this guideline as:

- Collection of practical tools and utilities when making decisions about digital technology
- Checklist for compliance with legal requirements
- Guide for implementing best practices
- Reference for evaluating existing systems
- Source of inspiration for responsibly deploying new technologies
- Toolkit with handles, templates and software tools for direct application

## Version 0.1

Version 0.1 is the first version of the Dutch Guideline for Digital Systems (NeRDS).
This version appeared on March 21, 2025.

In this first version, the emphasis is on setting up a framework on which we can build further.

development of new tools:

- Submit your [question or change](CONTRIBUTING.md).
- Contribute to the development of practical tools.
